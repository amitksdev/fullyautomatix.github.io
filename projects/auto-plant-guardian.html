Auto Plant Guardian

A self-watering node that tracks soil moisture and reservoir level, then actuates a pump relay inside a weather-safe 3D-printed housing.

Build Summary

This synthetic build targets balcony and indoor plants. Firmware runs a 2-minute sensing loop and logs moisture drift for calibration.

  • Capacitive moisture sensor with auto-zero calibration on boot
  • 12V mini pump driven by opto-isolated relay board
  • Two-piece PETG enclosure with gasket channel and cable gland
  • Status ring indicator: dry, watering, lockout, and service mode

Technical Specs
