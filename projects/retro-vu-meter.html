Retro VU Meter

A stereo line-level visualizer with analog front-end gain staging and a custom printed faceplate for classic instrument aesthetics.

Build Summary

This synthetic design uses dual channels with adjustable attack and decay constants for expressive needle response.

  • Dual op-amp preamp with trim calibration points
  • Selectable line-in sensitivity profile switch
  • Printed front bezel and knob caps in matte PETG
  • Backlight control for studio and low-light desk use

Technical Specs

Input3.5mm stereo line-in
Supply9V DC center-positive
DisplayDual analog needles + LED backlight
